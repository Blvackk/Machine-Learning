REGULARIZATION is the concept in which High coefficient is converted to Low coefficient
It is of 3 types :-
1. Ridge Regularization ==> l2 regression | l2 regularization
2. Lasso Regularization ==> l1 regression | l1 regularization
3. Elastic net ==> ( l1 + l2 )


Ridge or l2 --> Scale down High Coefficient to Low Coefficient to reduce the overfitting technique
   [ Ridge =  Loss + ||W||^2 (penalty) ]
this will bring coefficient up to 0  but not possibly 0


Lasso or l1  --> Scale down high coefficient to 0
  [ Lasso =  Loss + ||W|| (penalty) ]
this will bring coefficient to 0



Every ML model has inbuild parameter l1  & l2 --> very powerful
in GENERATIVE AI , Regularization concept is called " wd (WEIGHTED DECKEY)" 


Elastic Net  --> Loss + ||W||^2 + ||W||
